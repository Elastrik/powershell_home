1 - Be sure to have powershel V7 installed
2 - check at : architechture.txt to see how it works
3 - run instal.ps1 to create your profile
4 - update your $profile - check at /home/profile_example.txt to see what to put in you profile
5 - restart your terminal

6  - You can customize your profile folder in /home/<profile>/
7  - core.txt - the core functions you want to load
8  - app.txt - the app you want to load
9  -  /config for variables / function / Alias
10 - main.menu.json the mainmenu (mmm command)
11 - logo.ps1 is the welcome logo
12 - welcome there is the prompt definition
